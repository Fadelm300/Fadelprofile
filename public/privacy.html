Policy.

A clear explanation of what happens to the information you send through my portfolio contact form.

The short version

Your message is used only to reply.

No contact-form database, advertising profile, or mailing list is created.

Effective date

21 July 2026

Privacy contact fadel.m200@gmail.com
01

Information collected

The form collects your name, email address, optional phone number, and message. An IP address may be processed temporarily to limit spam and repeated submissions.

02

How it is used

Your information is used only to receive and respond to your inquiry. It is not used for advertising, profiling, newsletters, or unrelated marketing.

03

Delivery and storage

The website does not save submissions in a dedicated database. The backend is hosted by Vercel, and Brevo delivers the message to my email inbox. The email may remain there until it is no longer needed.

04

Sharing

Your personal information is not sold. It is processed only by the service providers needed to host the website and deliver your message.

05

Cookies

This site does not currently use advertising or analytics cookies.

06

Security and choices

The form uses HTTPS, validation, spam controls, and request limits. You may ask about, correct, or request deletion of information you submitted by emailing fadel.m200@gmail.com.

07

External links and updates

GitHub, LinkedIn, and other external websites apply their own privacy policies. This page may be updated when the portfolio or its services change.
